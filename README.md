# Simple Interest Calculator

A simple interest calculator to find the interest amount on a loan or investment.

## Formula
Simple Interest = (P × R × T) / 100

**Where:**
* **P** = Principal amount (the initial amount of money)
* **R** = Annual interest rate (in percentage)
* **T** = Time (in years)

## Example
If you borrow $1,000 at a 5% interest rate for 3 years:
* Simple Interest = (1000 × 5 × 3) / 100 = $150
